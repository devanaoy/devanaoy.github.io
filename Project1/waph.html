Hackathon-1 Overview

The XSS Hackathon, centered around Cross-site Scripting, serves as an educational initiative aimed at delving into and comprehending different tiers of XSS vulnerabilities within web applications. XSS, a prevalent security loophole, empowers attackers to infuse harmful scripts into web pages, visible to other users. The hackathon encompasses two primary objectives: Task 1 concentrates on recognizing and exploiting XSS vulnerabilities within a designated web application, whereas Task 2 centers on establishing protective measures against XSS attacks to diminish the likelihood of exploitation.
